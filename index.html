The Brand

Why Curio Collection by Hilton

Hilton's hand-picked portfolio of upper-upscale independent hotels. Each property keeps its own name, its own story, and its own identity, while plugging into the world's most powerful hospitality ecosystem.

01
Individually Branded

Every Curio hotel tells its own story. Hilton requires each property to have a unique name, identity, and design theme. No cookie-cutter layouts. This hotel will carry its own brand developed by Getty Design and Martin Branding, with restaurants and bars built around that narrative.

02
Hilton Honors Access

Direct access to Hilton Honors, one of the world's largest loyalty programs. This drives guaranteed booking volume and reduces dependency on high-commission OTAs (15-25% per booking). Corporate and group sales flow through Hilton's global distribution network.

03
Upper-Upscale Positioning

Curio sits between Hilton's luxury tier (LXR) and its standard upscale brands. These are four-to-five star properties that command premium ADR while reaching a broader market than ultra-luxury. Think sophisticated travelers who want character backed by reliability.

04
Curated Exclusivity

Hilton hand-selects properties for the Curio Collection. With only ~170 properties globally across 42 countries, scarcity is part of the brand's appeal. Each hotel is a destination, not just a place to stay.

Deal Structure

Investment Terms

GP/LP structure with preferred return and a waterfall promoting alignment between sponsor and investors.

Vehicle
Single-asset LLC. GP/LP structure. BW Development as General Partner with 10-20% co-investment alongside LP investors.
Minimum Investment
$100,000 minimum check size. Targeting family offices, high-net-worth individuals, and select accredited investors.
Preferred Return
LP investors receive a preferred return before any GP promote, ensuring downside alignment and priority cash distributions.
Waterfall
Multi-tier waterfall structure at stabilization recap or sale. GP promote only kicks in after LP preferred return and return of capital.
Target Hold Period
4-year hold with exit via recapitalization or asset sale. Construction begins September 2026, target stabilization March 2028, exit October 2030.
Exit Strategy
Stabilized Curio-flagged assets trade at institutional cap rates. Target 7.04% exit cap with $89.0M gross sale price at Year 4.
